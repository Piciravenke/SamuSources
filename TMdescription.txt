The 3 tales are made from just BE words (BASIC ENGLISH: https://simple.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Basic_English_ordered_wordlist).
They're suitable for a 5 years old. You can find 49 triplets on it.
The y1 diary is made from words that a typical 1 year child is using. You can find 12 triplets on it.
The y2 diary is made from words that a typical 2 year child is using. You can find 19 triplets on it.
